Please Note:
1. sat and gt directories contains satellite images and their corresponding ground truth values in .tif format
2. You may use the QGIS open source software to visually explore the data
3. The satellite images in sat directory are composed of channels - R (Band 1),G (Band 2),B (Band 3) and NIR (Band 4)
4. The ground truth images in gt directory depict 8 classes - Roads, Buildings, Trees, Grass, Bare Soil, Water, Railways and Swimming pools
